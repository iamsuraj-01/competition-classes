About the school

Navodaya is unique in many respects compared to other Navodaya Schools in the country. Main features are mentioned below:

  • 1. It is the First Navodaya School of India, established in 1960.
  • 2. Admissions are taken in Class 6 and Class 9.
  • 3. Admission is open only to domicile students of Uttar Pradesh.
  • 4. It has started giving admissions to girl students. Last year, girl students were given admission in Class 9.
  • 5. It conducts its Entrance Exam separately and not with the other Navodaya Schools. The exam is conducted in the month of January.
  • 6. It conducts interviews after the Written Test.
Recent Updates :
  • 1. A new Navodaya School has been added in Gorakhpur.
  • 2. Girls & Boys can apply to Class 9 in Navodaya School.
  • 3. Only Boys can apply to Class 6 in Lucknow Navodaya School.
  • 4. Boys & Girls can apply to Class 6 & 9 in Gorakhpur Navodaya School.
