About the location

Situated in the tranquil village of Shyampur, near the spiritually enriching city of Rishikesh, our school offers a peaceful ambiance that fosters a dynamic learning environment. Shyampur, with its proximity to Rishikesh and the revered Ganges River, provides a serene backdrop that greatly enhances the educational experience. The area is well-connected by road and offers easy access to various essential amenities, making it convenient for day scholars and visitors.

The campus is equipped with modern facilities, including state-of-the-art science laboratories, comprehensive sports complexes, and a recently upgraded library that supports both the academic and extracurricular activities of our students.

Accessibility :
  • Nearest airport: Jolly Grant Airport, 25 km away
  • Nearest railway station: Rishikesh Railway Station, 10 km away
Local Attractions :
  • Numerous ashrams and yoga centers, including Parmarth Niketan
  • Rajaji National Park, offering wildlife and scenic beauty
